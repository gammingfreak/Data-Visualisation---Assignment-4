Yelp Academic Dataset - Data Visualisation


  • Donut chart of states by Business Index*

The donut chat helps to identify the states with higest business index. Therefore we focus the data analysis to the top two businesses i.e. (Arizona and Nevada)
*Business Index = SUM(Stars of the businesses)/SUM(ReviewCount)


  • Sankey Chart - Flow of Business Index from States - Cities - Categories

The Sankey chart shows the flow of the Business Index* from State to City to the Categories of businesses
*Business Index = SUM(Stars of the businesses)/SUM(ReviewCount)


  • Group Bar Graph of Categories and States

Group Bar graph of categories to compare Arizona against Nevada in the number of businesses in each state
